Brand identity proposition for a seaside craft beer taproom.

(01) BRAND
(02) MOTION
(03) PRINT
(04) STRATEGY

Love letter to the mythical past and life of the southern English coastline, inspired by its' history and the diverse elements of nature.

Combining the element of craft through lino-cut illustrations and hand-written notes with sophisticated colours and sentimental imagery to celebrate the beauty of the English coastline. The brand establishes a clear, unique presence within the taproom market.
